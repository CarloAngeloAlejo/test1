Junior High School

Grade 7 to 10

Junior high school is a crucial educational stage that bridges the gap between primary education and high school. During these formative years, students typically explore a broad range of subjects, building a foundation in core disciplines such as mathematics, science, language arts, and social studies. This phase focuses on academic and personal development, emphasizing critical thinking, problem-solving, and effective communication. Junior high school plays a vital role in preparing students for the challenges of high school and beyond, equipping them with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in their academic journey and personal growth.

Skills to be Aquired

    • Academic Foundations
    • Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving Skills
    • Effective Communication Skills
    • Subject Specialization
    • Cultural and Global Awareness
    • Values and Ethics
    • Interpersonal Skills
    • Physical Fitness
    • Artistic and Creative Expression
    • Career Exploration
    • Preparation for High School
    • Preparation for Future Goals
