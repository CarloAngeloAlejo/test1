Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM)

SENIOR HIGHSCHOOL DEPARTMENT

ABM equips students with a comprehensive education in accountancy, business, and management. The curriculum covers key areas including accounting, economics, marketing, and organizational management. Graduates develop financial acumen, strategic thinking, and leadership skills. This program prepares students for diverse career opportunities in finance, business management, and entrepreneurship.

The ABM program fosters financial acumen, strategic thinking, and leadership skills, making graduates well-prepared for careers in finance, business management, and entrepreneurship. It provides a solid foundation for diverse career opportunities in the fields of accountancy, business, and management.
