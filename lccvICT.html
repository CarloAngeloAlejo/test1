Information and Communication Technology (ICT)

SENIOR HIGHSCHOOL DEPARTMENT

An ICT (Information and Communication Technology) program is an educational curriculum designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of information technology and communication systems. Covering core concepts such as computer systems, programming, networking, and database management, the program aims to cultivate skills in software development, web technologies, cybersecurity, and IT project management. Students engage in practical experiences, hands-on projects, and may explore emerging technologies, preparing them for diverse career paths like systems analysis, database administration, software development, network administration, and cybersecurity analysis. Effective communication skills are emphasized, recognizing their significance in the professional realm.
