Vision and Mission

Vision & Mission

Moved by the Augistinian Spirit, we, the community of La Consolacio College Valenzuela, envision ourselves as Christ's disciples, living His mission by providing relevant and responsive academic and co-curricular programs and active involvement in community service towards social transformation for the realization of the reign of God.

Core Values

UNITY. CHARITY. TRUTH.

Encapsulates a commitment to collaboration, kindness, and honesty as core values.
