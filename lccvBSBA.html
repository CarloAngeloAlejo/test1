Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

COLLEGE DEPARTMENT

The BSBA program shapes future business leaders through a comprehensive curriculum covering essential business principles, management, and key subjects such as marketing, finance, human resources, and operations. Graduates emerge well-prepared for a range of business roles, with a strong focus on ethical and sustainable practices. This degree serves as the cornerstone for a successful career in business administration, offering pathways to excel in the dynamic business world.

The BSBA program equips students with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in the ever-evolving business landscape. With a curriculum that blends practical expertise and essential soft skills, graduates are poised to lead responsibly while promoting ethical and sustainable practices. This program opens doors to a fulfilling and impactful career in business administration.
