General Academic Strand (GAS)

SENIOR HIGHSCHOOL DEPARTMENT

The GAS program offers a well-rounded education encompassing various academic disciplines. Students explore subjects such as literature, mathematics, science, social studies, and the arts. This diverse curriculum promotes critical thinking, effective communication, and a broad knowledge base. Graduates are equipped for versatile career paths and further studies, providing a solid foundation for personal and professional growth.

GAS encourages students to develop a well-rounded perspective by engaging with a wide range of academic disciplines. This program prepares them for careers and higher education, fostering skills and knowledge that are valuable in various fields and endeavors.
