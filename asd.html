Welcome LCCians!

La Consolacion College Valenzuela (formerly St. Jude Academy) is proud of its scholastic achievements and community services. It owes its existence to the generous donation of land by Don Pastor Constantino of Polo, Valenzuela City. In 1963, he donated 3,000 square meters of land to the Augustinian Sisters of the Philippines (ASP), now known as the Augustinian Sisters of Our Lady of Consolacion (ASOLC). This marked the beginning of a relevant institution, which became the instrument of God to sow the seeds of living faith of the compassionate Christ in a community of friends.
