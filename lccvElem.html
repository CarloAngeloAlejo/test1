Elementary

Kindergarten to Grade 6

Grade school represents the initial years of formal education for children, typically spanning from kindergarten to fifth or sixth grade. During this foundational period, students acquire fundamental skills in subjects like mathematics, language arts, science, and social studies. The curriculum aims to cultivate basic literacy and numeracy, along with introducing critical thinking and problem-solving. Grade school provides a solid educational base, promoting essential life skills, fostering a love for learning, and helping children establish a strong academic and social foundation. It is a critical phase in a student's educational journey, laying the groundwork for future academic success and personal growth.

    • Social Skills
