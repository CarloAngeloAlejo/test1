Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

COLLEGE DEPARTMENT

The BSHM program shapes future leaders in the dynamic hospitality industry, focusing on hospitality principles, business management, and customer service excellence. Students explore hotel and restaurant management, event planning, tourism, and food service operations, gaining practical skills and honing critical thinking and communication abilities. Graduates are well-prepared for roles in hotel management, event coordination, restaurant operations, and tourism management, emphasizing ethical and sustainable practices.

The BSHM degree unlocks a fulfilling career in one of the world's most dynamic industries. With a comprehensive curriculum that blends practical expertise and holistic skills, graduates are ready to lead responsibly in the field while embracing ethical and sustainable practices. The BSHM program is your gateway to a rewarding journey in hospitality management.
