Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

SENIOR HIGHSCHOOL DEPARTMENT

HUMSS provides students with a well-rounded education in humanities and social sciences, covering literature, philosophy, history, psychology, sociology, and political science. Graduates gain critical thinking, communication skills, and an understanding of human behavior and society. This program prepares students for diverse career paths, making a positive impact on communities and society.

The HUMSS program fosters empathy, critical analysis, and effective communication by exploring human culture and society. It opens doors to fulfilling careers in understanding and addressing the complex challenges and opportunities within the humanities and social sciences.

  • Social Sciences Courses:
